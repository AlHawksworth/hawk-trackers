📐 Kelly Criterion Explained

The Kelly Criterion calculates optimal bet sizing based on your edge and the odds offered:

f* = (bp - q) / b

Where b = decimal odds - 1, p = true probability of winning, q = 1 - p.

We use Quarter Kelly by default to reduce variance while maintaining positive expected value.
